# JARVIS Sportivi Dashboard — Product Context

## Product Purpose
An AI-powered affiliate marketing command center for "Sportivi For You" — a Telegram channel selling AliExpress sports products to Israeli Hebrew-speaking consumers. JARVIS automates product discovery, scoring, content creation, and publishing through 8 named AI agents. The dashboard is the human operator's window into the system.

## Register
product

## Users
Single primary user: Moti Shimoni, a solo entrepreneur running the affiliate operation. He checks this dashboard several times a day to monitor agent activity, track revenue, spot top-performing products, and read AI recommendations. He is not a developer. He wants to feel in control of a sophisticated automated system.

## Brand tone
Tactical. Precise. Intelligent. The interface should feel like a war room for commerce — not a SaaS analytics tool. The emotional register is "I built something powerful and I understand what it's doing." Hebrew RTL throughout.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS dashboards (Mixpanel, Amplitude clone aesthetics)
- "Dark mode startup" — teal gradients, Inter font, rounded cards
- Crypto dashboard clichés — neon green on black, coin tickers
- Corporate BI tools — grey grids, Arial, Excel aesthetics

## Strategic principles
1. The system should feel alive — agents are characters, not rows in a table
2. Data is secondary to narrative — surface what matters, not everything
3. Confidence over density — fewer numbers presented with conviction beats many numbers in noise
4. Hebrew-first — all labels, alerts, and copy in Hebrew; system identifiers (agent names, KPIs) in English caps
